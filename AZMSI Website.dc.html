ABOUT AZMSI

A new kind of medical institution — built for the era of AI and innovation.

Arizona Medical Sciences Institute (AZMSI) is a fully online executive medical sciences institution dedicated to non-clinical advanced medical education — bridging medicine, technology, artificial intelligence, healthcare operations, research & development, and executive leadership.

Unlike traditional medical schools focused on clinical physician licensure, AZMSI delivers executive-level education for professionals seeking leadership, innovation, research, and healthcare transformation roles.

DEGREE PROGRAM

The Executive Medical Doctorate (eMD)

An advanced non-clinical executive medical sciences degree combining medical sciences, AI medicine, healthcare leadership, executive strategy, translational medicine, biotechnology, and research methodologies — across 12 quarters of structured online study.

ADMISSIONS

Who should apply

AZMSI welcomes healthcare professionals, executives, engineers, scientists, researchers, innovators, and entrepreneurs seeking innovation-focused, non-clinical medical sciences education.

1.
AZMSI Admissions Qualification Examination (AQE)
A 2,000-question adaptive bank evaluating scientific literacy, medical terminology, critical thinking, and research aptitude.
2.
Application review

3.
Professional background evaluation
Review of professional experience and leadership trajectory.
4.
Admissions interview
Conducted when required.
